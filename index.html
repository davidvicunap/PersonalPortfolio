About Me

Finance and AI Professional

At Apple, I advise SMB clients on complex technology deployments: evaluating organizational needs, structuring solutions across hardware, software, and services, and guiding executive stakeholders through high-stakes procurement decisions. It's consultative, relationship-driven work that mirrors the advisory mindset at the core of the vision and purpose of my work.

In parallel, I've been building deep competence in equity research and financial analysis. Three of my independent research reports; published through my Substack, Investment Edge; were picked up and syndicated by Yahoo Finance. I'm actively preparing for the CFA Level I exam while developing skills in technical analysis, financial modeling, and business valuation and corporate strategy.

What sets me apart is the intersection: enterprise technology fluency, hands-on AI expertise (certified by both Google), and a disciplined, research-first approach to financial markets. In a landscape where teams increasingly need members who understand both the numbers and the technology driving them, I bring both.
